Careful Resume

This is a congestion control mechanism to be used with a congestion control algorithm.

Implementation Experience with CR
=================================

The algorithm has been implemented in:
1. a fork of CF Quiche (Ana Custura & Mihail Yanev).
2. a for of Picoquic (Joerg Deustchman & Matthias Hofstaette).
3. a prototype implementation in quicly (Kazuho).

Interworking was demonstrated in Nov 2024 from 1,2 with clients using Chrome, Firefox and Safari.

Last updated 1 Dec 2024.
